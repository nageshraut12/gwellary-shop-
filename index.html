Our Legacy

Craftsmanship Rooted
in Tradition

Founded in 1985, Mahalaxmi Jewellers has been Nagpur's most trusted name in fine jewellery for over three decades. What began as a modest showroom on Sitabuldi has grown into a legacy that thousands of families have made part of their most cherished moments.

Every piece we create carries forward generations of craftsmanship — from intricate Kolhapuri designs to contemporary solitaire settings, our master karigars pour their hearts into every gram of gold.
